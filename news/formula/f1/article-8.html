🏆 Verstappen Strikes Back in Bahrain FP2
April 11, 2025

Max Verstappen topped FP2 at the 2025 Bahrain Grand Prix, setting the fastest lap under Sakhir’s floodlights. The Red Bull driver edged out McLaren’s Norris in cooler 30°C conditions, showing strong pace after FP1’s rookie run. His lap signals Red Bull’s intent, but McLaren remains close. FP2’s night session offered better qualifying data, setting up a tight battle ahead.
