🔴 Hamilton Grabs Third in Bahrain FP1
April 11, 2025

Lewis Hamilton kicked off his Ferrari chapter with a gritty P3 in FP1 at the 2025 Bahrain Grand Prix, posting a lap 0.596 seconds off Lando Norris’s pace. The seven-time champ, sporting Ferrari’s upgraded floor, showed flashes of speed but wasn’t all smiles, grumbling over the radio about a “twitchy” car that’s proving tough to tame. Hamilton’s move to Maranello has fans buzzing, but this session highlighted setup challenges, especially in Sakhir’s 46°C heat, which stressed tires and balance. With teammate Charles Leclerc sidelined for rookie Dino Beganovic, Hamilton carried Ferrari’s hopes alone, landing a decent but not dominant result. As cooler conditions loom for FP2, Ferrari’s engineers will burn the midnight oil to unlock more pace. Can Hamilton turn his P3 into a front-row fight by qualifying?
