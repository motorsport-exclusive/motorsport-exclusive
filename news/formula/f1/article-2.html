🔵 Gasly’s Alpine Surprise in Bahrain FP1
April 11, 2025

Pierre Gasly stole the show in FP1 at the 2025 Bahrain Grand Prix, piloting his Alpine to a stellar P2, just 0.238 seconds shy of Lando Norris’s chart-topping 1:33.204. The Frenchman, often a midfield maestro, looked right at home on the Sakhir track, capitalizing on Alpine’s aggressive setup to outpace heavyweights like Ferrari and Red Bull. Gasly’s radio crackled with optimism, noting “the car’s got real pace today.” This performance raises hopes for Alpine, who’ve struggled for consistency since their 2021 win in Hungary. With teammate Jack Doohan also in the top 10, the team’s upgrades seem to be clicking, but the hotter FP1 conditions—track temps over 46°C—may flatter their pace. As night falls for qualifying, Gasly will need to dig deep to keep Alpine in the fight.
