🟠 Norris Sets the Pace in Bahrain FP1
April 11, 2025

Lando Norris sent a bold message to his rivals in the 2025 Bahrain Grand Prix’s opening practice, torching the Sakhir circuit with a stunning 1:33.204 to claim P1. The McLaren star, fresh off a strong 2024, looked untouchable, gapping Alpine’s Pierre Gasly by 0.238 seconds. Norris credited his team’s upgraded MCL39, saying post-session, “The car feels alive here.” With McLaren chasing a constructors’ title, this early dominance suggests they’re ready to fight. However, daytime conditions in FP1, with track temps hitting 46°C, don’t fully mirror the cooler night sessions ahead, so Norris’s pace will face a sterner test in FP2. Rivals like Ferrari and Red Bull, who ran conservative programs, will likely close the gap, but for now, Norris and McLaren are the ones to beat in the desert.
